--- title: '«Happy» World IP Day 2014!' author: derechoaleer created: !!timestamp 2014-04-26 11:00:00 ntags: - Copyfight - Free Culture type: story status: 1 comments_status: open --- {% mark excerpt -%} [*"Movies, a global passion"*](https://www.facebook.com/worldipday) is the slogan of the global event sponsored by the [World Intellectual Property Organization](http://www.wipo.int) (WIPO) called [**"World IP Day"**](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Intellectual_Property_Day) ([#worldIPDay](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23WorldIPDay&src=hash) on twitter). The celebration is hold annually every April 26. It seems that if you're passionate about movies, you should become a sort of defender of [*"intellectual property"*](https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/words-to-avoid.html#IntellectualProperty). However, we believe that the defenders of "intellectual property" --particularly the most extreme ones-- are nourished by certain and often unspeakable passions. In order to promote (a real) *"discussion of the role of intellectual property (IP) in encouraging innovation and creativity"* we celebrate by posting some cartoons, that are a remix of the characters created (and free released) by [Nina Paley](http://mimiandeunice.com/).
